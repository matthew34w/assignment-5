We bring the party to climbing!

Here at ascent climbing gym we strive in creating a fun and competitive atmosphere that is enjoyable for everyone.We offer a wide range climbing routes for every difficulty level.

Thinking about improving your skill?

We have climbing training programs aimed at making you a stronger climber. We also have belay classes and lead climbing classes to help bring your slills to the next level. You can ask any of our staff and they will provide you will more detailed information.

Looking to compete?

We host member climbing competitions every 3 months to create a friendly and competitive atmostphere. We also host competitions with other gyms across the seattle area. You will also find that we have cash prizes for the winners.

Bring the kids!

We encourage all ages to come to our gym and we have many activities for children to engage in. We also have a play area for the ones a little too young for the walls.
